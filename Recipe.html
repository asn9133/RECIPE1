Chicken Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Spinach
  • Chicken Breast
  • Celery
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salad dressing
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Chop chicken breast into small pieces
  2. Put pan on stove
  3. Put olive oil in pan and let it warm
  4. Put chicken pieces into pan
  5. Sprinke some salt,pepper, and garlic powder on the chicken
  6. Saute the chiceken for a few minutes
  7. Turn of the stove and keep the chicken aside
  8. Put kale in the bowl
  9. Put spinach in the bowl
  10. Chop the celery and put it in the bowl
  11. Put the cooked chicken pieces in the bowl
  12. Put some shredded cheese in the bowl
  13. Put cherry tomatoes ib the bowl
  14. Pour some salad dressing on the salad
  15. Mix the contents of the bowl and serve
